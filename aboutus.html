About us

The LSE Law Review

The LSE Law Review is a student-run law journal seeking to provide a platform for high quality legal scholarship, with the ultimate aim of contributing to debates surrounding all areas of the law. Our Editorial Board is composed of LSE students from all years of study who are fully responsible for all editorial and organisational decisions in relation to the Review’s publication. We work independently, albeit with the endorsement, of the LSE Department of Law.

2018 Launch Night

The LSE Law Review was pleased to present Volume III at its annual Launch Night on Monday, March 12th 2018.

The evening celebrated the third edition of the Review and included speeches from honorary guest speaker Sir Robin Jacob and Professor Andrew Murray (LSE LL.B. Director), and an awards presentation for outstanding submissions.

2017 Launch Night

The LSE Law Review was delighted to hold its inaugural Launch Night on Tuesday 22nd March 2017.

The evening celebrated the publication of the first edition of the Review. The event included speeches from keynote speaker Cherie Blair QC and Andrew Murray (LLB Director), and an awards presentation for outstanding submissions.
