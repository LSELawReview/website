Our Sponsors
Founded 10 years ago as the UK’s first undergraduate law journal, the OUULJ is independent and entirely student-run. The journal is Oxford’s only publication for undergraduate legal writing. Its Honorary Board comprises distinguished members such as Lord Neuberger and Lord Phillips as well as various notable legal academics and professionals. The Journal appears in print as well as on HeinOnline and LexisNexis, and submissions are accepted on a rolling basis from undergraduate students (and recent graduates) at all universities.\nFurther details can be found at:\nwww.law.ox.ac.uk/ouulj.\nEnquiries should be addressed to: ouulj@law.ox.ac.uk.
